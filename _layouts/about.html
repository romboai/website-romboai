Our Mission and Expertise

About Us
Fast, Accurate, AI-Powered NMR Spectral Analysis

Rombo AI is an Italian deep-tech startup founded in 2023 and headquartered in Cagliari. We develop advanced Artificial Intelligence solutions for spectral analysis, helping industries overcome one of the most persistent bottlenecks in chemical and material characterization: slow, expert-dependent laboratory analysis.

Our team is composed of PhDs in Machine Learning, Physics, and Mathematics, with deep expertise in software development and Spectroscopy. This unique combination allows us to design AI-driven solutions that are both scientifically rigorous and practically applicable.

Our Vision

To redefine chemical industrial analysis by making the combination of Artificial Intelligence and NMR seamlessly accessible, enabling businesses worldwide to innovate with precision, speed, and AI confidence.

Our Mission

We empower medium and large enterprises – especially in energy, oil & gas, biotech, and chemical industries – with fast, precise, and automated molecular characterization and quantification. By combining cutting-edge Low Field NMR spectroscopy with our patented Artificial Intelligence models, we deliver end-to-end automated solutions that reduce manual work, accelerate R&D breakthroughs, and set new standards for efficiency.
