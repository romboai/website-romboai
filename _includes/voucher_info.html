The Rombo AI NMR Analyzer is an advanced solution for crude oil analysis that integrates benchtop Low Field Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) technology with AI models. Designed for the oil and gas industry, it provides detailed, real-time insights into the chemical composition of crude oil and refined products. This non-destructive technique ensures precise, rapid reporting in less than 15 minutes, aiding process optimization and cost reduction. With Machine Learning models, it allows operators to monitor quality, predict outcomes, and improve operational efficiency​​.
